# Partner Portals

A Partner Portal provides partners with access to deal registration, marketing resources, pricing and sales information for products and services, as well as technical details and support.

## Target Audience

The key stakeholders for a Partner Portal reflect the structure of the company, but the decision maker is typically high in the sales organization such as the Chief Sales Office (CSO) or the leader of the sales or channels team.  The IT team will also contribute to the decision.

* CSO
* Head of, Director of, VP of, Manager
  * Sales
  * Channels
* CIO/Head of IT

Partner Portals provide many benefits for both the host of the portal as well as the external partners.
