# Digital Commerce sites

A Digital Commerce Site allows people to buy physical goods, services, and digital products over the internet.

## Target Audience

The buying audience for Digital Commerce will typically be from both the Marketing and Sales organizations.  With many integration requirements IT will almost always be heavily involved:

* CMO
* CRO
* Head of, Director of, VP of, Manager
  * Marketing
  * Digital Experience
  * Digital Commerce
  * Business Development,
  * Digital Channels
  * eCommerce
* CIO/Head of IT

## Key Benefits

Using Liferay DXP as a basis for a digital commerce site provides several key benefits that we will want to highlight when talking to prospective customers:

* An effective digital commerce site should **make it easy to shop online**. They help customers discover the right products and ensure fit and compatibility.  Liferay DXP provides value through native integration between content management and commerce capabilities as well as powerful search capabilities
* Good digital commerce sites are more than a product catalog. They also **simplify ordering** even with complex ordering rules or complex products.  This is particularly common with B2B but is also sometimes a factor with B2C.   Once orders are placed, **follow-up is simplified** with order and shipment status visibility for customers
* Some sales teams see a digital commerce site as competition for them. But digital commerce should **assist sales and service teams** by relieving them of mundane tasks easily accomplished through self-service - freeing them up to focus on higher value tasks and interactions
* A seamless and intuitive commerce experience will bring customers back for additional purchases and Liferayâ€™s merchandising and discounting capabilities can **increase order size and frequency**
* Finally, Liferay DXP helps customers **grow and scale efficiently**
