# Customer Portals

A Customer Portal is a website that provides customers with a single point of access to all relevant information about the products and services they are purchasing from a vendor.

A key point here is that the customer or the end user of the products or services provided by the organization are the primary users of the solution.

## Target Audience

The primary buying audience for a Customer Portal will be from Customer Success, Sales, Operations or Marketing.  IT will also typically be involved:

* Head of Customer Success/Experience
* Head of Operations
* CFO/VP Finance
* CMO/VP of Marketing
* CIO/Head of IT

## Key Benefits

Each organization has their own unique needs, but there are some key benefits of a Customer Portal that should resonate with all audiences:

* A Customer Portal allows you to **Streamline Onboarding** and provide a personalized experience that help customers get the most out the relationship
* Customer Portals **Empower Customers** with Account Management Capabilities and Self Service so they can handle common business tasks such as making routine purchases, making payments, and managing their account information
* Customer Portals can also **Help Customers Find Answers** and solve their own problems
* Customers expect to be able to conduct business on their own terms, and a customer portal helps you to **Provide 24/7 Availability**
